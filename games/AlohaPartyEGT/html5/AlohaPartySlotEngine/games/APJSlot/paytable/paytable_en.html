FREE SPINS

3 or more left to right symbols on consecutive reels, begining with the leftmost reel trigger 12 FREE SPINS.  

During the FREE SPINS:

If symbol participates in a win combination on the 2nd reel, this win combination will be multiplied x3.

If symbol participates in a win combination on the 4th reel, this win combination will be multiplied x5.

If symbol participates in a win combination on the 2nd and on the 4th reel, this win combination will be multiplied x15.

If symbol appears on the 2nd and/or on the 4th reel(s) on active positions on the reels and does not participate in a win combination, the reel(s) with the symbol(s) is(are) held and all other reels make one free respin.

 

In case of retriggering of the FREE SPINS, the player wins 12 new FREE SPINS, which are added to the current number of FREE SPINS. The FREE SPINS are played at trigger REEL WAYS PAY bet and bet multiplier.

JACKPOT CARDS is a four-level mystery jackpot. Each mystery jackpot level is illustrated by a card suit:
(CLUBS) - 1st level (the lowest jackpot value); (DIAMONDS) - 2nd level; (HEARTS) - 3rd level;
(SPADES) - 4th and the highest level.
Any of the jackpot levels can be won during the JACKPOT CARDS Mystery bonus which is randomly triggered after a single game is finished and all resulting winnings are collected.
The player selects cards from the pick field made up of 12 cards face-down until revealing 3 matching card suit symbols. The JACKPOT CARDS Mystery level corresponding to the revealed suit will be awarded. The bonus amount will correspond to the amount accumulated at the moment of revealing the last matching symbol, shown on the right side of each card suit.
The player has a reasonable chance to win the highest level of the jackpot with any of the qualifying bets, every time when he/she enters the JACKPOT CARDS bonus.
Qualifying bets: 100 - 500

REEL WAYS PAY RULES

 

The REEL WAYS PAY Bet multiplied by the bet multiplier is equal to the total bet.
The GREY symbol positions are excluded from REEL WAYS PAY Play.
Scatter symbols trigger the free spins feature even if they are on excluded for play positions.
Win combinations may contain only one symbol in an enabled position from each consecutive reel.
Win combinations are paid left to right on adjacent reels, on enabled positions, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays the same win combination again. The pays of win combinations are multiplied by the number of the bet multiplier. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet value. Only the highest win combination and/or scatter wins are paid.
Win combinations may be paid multiple times. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
